About me:

My name is Anthony Golovin, I was born an raised in the United States of America although the rest of my family was actually born in Russia. My initial interest in technology came from a very young age through hobbies like gaming. As I grew older I found myself lacking direction and passion which stopped me from going to college as I did not know what I was going to do, at the time I was working at AT&T sales and it clicked for me that I wanted to be involved in technology and not sell it. I quickly got my foot in the door by starting to work at 343 Industries to do QA work on Halo: Infinte and about 9 months later I realized I wanted to go deeper and get more involved in development.
